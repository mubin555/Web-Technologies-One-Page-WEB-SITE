Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona is one of Spain’s most vibrant cities, renowned for its unique architecture, delicious cuisine, and rich culture. It is home to FC Barcelona and iconic landmarks like the Sagrada Família.

The city blends historic charm with modern style, making it a top destination for travelers worldwide.

Learn more on Wikipedia | Official Tourism Site

  • Barcelona was founded as a Roman city.
  • The Sagrada Família has been under construction since 1882.
  • Camp Nou is one of the largest football stadiums in the world.
